For: BID
With: CEEU

  • Federico Catalano - Data Science

A new second bridge was near being started to connect the cities of Resistencia and Corrientes. Because there was no study and nobody knew the consecuences of the new bridge, we're asked to evaluate the socio-economic impact of it.

Process

Data Adquisition

For the analysis that were needed to produce, differeent types of data was adquired. Sentinel 2 images were downloaded from the Copernicus hub and Landsat images from the USGS page; census data was downloaded from the INDEC page; neighborhoods distribution of both cities were digitalized; hidrological and roads data was downloaded from the IGN's services.

Urban Growth

Sentinel and Landsat images were classify to generate the urban footprints of each cities, for three different years. With this data was posibly to determine the size of each city every year and how cities grew from year to year.

Topographic Model

Using a dem model and layer of rivers and lakes, a topographic map was created to analysis the low level zones of the cities.

Disimilitud and Correlarion Index

To understand the socio-economic structure and distribution of both cities, a disimilitud index was calculated with the censal radio data, to create clusters of socio-economic groups.
Also a Local Moran's I index was created, to analize the cluster and outliers of the economic distribution in each city.

Report

With all the data and maps generated, a report was delivered, with all the methodologies and processes developed, and the analysis and conclusions of the urban experts.
