Road images dataset exploration
With: IGEO
For: Corredores Viales S.A.

Around 2.4 million images were available corresponding to 3,500km of national routes, corresponding to sections I to VI, and the quality of this data set was needed to be known, because it would be used for future applications and analysis.

Merging

The 2.4 million images were grouped into groups of 2000 images, separated by section, route, direction and camera; each group with a metadata csv file.

All the images were copied to a single location and the files were merged into one.

Exploration

Correction of incorrect values, due to errors in the data or values ​​in different units.

Analysis of grouped information by sections, routes, directions and cameras, to, for example, know the number of images per route.

Analysis of information from image files and csv data, and the crossing of these two, for example, to determine missing images.

Report

Charts were generated for the report, along with photos and tables, to facilitate the understanding of the data and results.

A report was written detailing the analysis carried out, the results found, and the conclusions reached, proposing actions to be taken.
