Processing and visualization of census data
Para: Nilus

Some way was required to be able to determine if the objectives of the company were being met. It was decided to make graphs with which to determine this situation, or if, on the contrary, the tasks that were being carried out were far from the vision of the company.

Data collection and processing

Socio-economic data were necessary for the analysis and decision making, for which INDEC census data were downloaded through REDATAM.

The data of the delivery points, on which a cleaning was carried out, were provided by Nilus.

The data of the delivery points were crossed with the socioeconomic data, such as unemployment, educational level, quality of housing, etc.

Data visualization

In order to visualize the spatial distribution of the points with respect to the different socio-economic variables, maps were made.

Histograms, scatter plots and bar graphs were made to be able to visualize the distribution of a variable of the delivery points or to compare some variables against others, always with respect to the total census radios.
