Topographic survey for landscaping project
For: Estudio Thays

The project included the design of the park, the construction of a country house and a tennis court, which required a survey of the existing objects to define the correct location of the perimeter fences.
A topographic survey was carried out, on which the project was adjusted, and finally the stakes were placed in the vertices of the property.

Survey

A survey of the site was carried out, using a Trimble M3 Total Station, for which initially a reference system materialized from four stakes was built. Some buildings measurements were made with tape measure.

After the survey was completed, the data was downloaded and a map of the existing events was made, which was used by the designer to adjust the project.

Stakeout

After adjusting the project, a stakeout of each of the vertices of the fences and gates to be placed was carried out, materializing each one with a stake.

The wooden stakes of 5cmx5cm by 120cm high, had the name assigned according to the plan written on the surface, and a plastic sheet attached to them.
